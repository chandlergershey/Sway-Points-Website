How Sway Points works

Sway Points is a Slack application that gives team members the ability to have weighted votes on matters they value the most. Typical voting applications give members one vote per issue. Now team members on Slack are given a number of votes per week and they can pick and choose which issues they would like to have more voting influence over.

Use Sway Points in your workspace

Enterprises, students, and teams can use Sway Points in Slack channels, currently at no cost.
